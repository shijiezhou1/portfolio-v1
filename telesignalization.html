Telesignalization

Telesignalization, a machine predicting intimacy level, was used to measure the different stages of a relationship based on how far a person stand next to them. Usually, most of us do not pay attention to the boundary between ourself and others. The inspiration for the telesignalization machine is the surveillance camera, which plays the role of an advance fortune-telling machine.
